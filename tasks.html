About this Page

This page outlines the usability testing scenarios and tasks completed by participants to evaluate the functionality and overall user experience of PawTrack.

Task and Scenarios

We prepared seven tasks and scenarios for our participants to evaluate the usability and overall experience of the PawTrack application.

Scenario:

You are a pet owner who wants to check the vaccination status of your pets and review their upcoming vaccinations.

Tasks:
  1. Open the PawTrack application
  2. Navigate to the Vaccination Records screen
  3. View the list of your registered pets
  4. Check the vaccination status of each pet
  5. Review the last vaccination date
  6. Switch between Current and Past Records tabs
  7. Identify any upcoming vaccinations
Scenario:

One of your pets needs a vaccination. You want to schedule an appointment with a veterinary clinic using the app.

Tasks:
  1. Navigate to the Book Appointment screen
  2. Select a pet from the list
  3. Choose a clinic
  4. Select service type (vaccination, check-up, etc.)
  5. Set the appointment date
  6. Set the appointment time
  7. Click Confirm Appointment
  8. Verify the appointment was booked successfully
Scenario:

You recently got a new pet and want to register it so you can track health records and appointments.

Tasks:
  1. Navigate to Add New Pet
  2. Upload a photo of your pet
  3. Enter the pet name
  4. Select the species
  5. Input the breed
  6. Set the date of birth
  7. Add optional notes
  8. Click Save
  9. Verify the pet appears in the system
Scenario:

After booking an appointment, you want to confirm that it appears in your records.

Tasks:
  1. Return to Vaccination Records
  2. Check the Upcoming Vaccinations section
  3. Locate the scheduled appointment
  4. Verify details such as pet name, date, clinic, and service
Scenario:

You want to review the complete vaccination history of your pet.

Tasks:
  1. Navigate to Vaccination Records
  2. Switch to Past Records
  3. Select a pet
  4. View detailed vaccination information
    • Service type
    • Date administered
    • Clinic name
  5. Scroll through past records
Scenario:

You want to explore different sections of the app using the bottom navigation menu.

Tasks:
  1. Use the bottom navigation bar
  2. Go to Home
  3. Navigate to Records
  4. Open Reminders
  5. Access Profile
  6. Return to the previous screen
Scenario:

You want to update your pet’s profile picture.

Tasks:
  1. Navigate to pet profile or Add New Pet
  2. Click Upload Photo
  3. Select an image
  4. Confirm the selected photo
  5. Verify the image updates successfully
